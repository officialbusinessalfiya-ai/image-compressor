How It Works
You select or drop an image — it's read locally using the FileReader API.
The image is drawn onto an off-screen HTML5 Canvas element.
canvas.toBlob() re-encodes the image at your chosen quality and format.
The resulting Blob is converted to an object URL and offered as a download.
Zero network requests are made at any point during this process.

1. Data We Collect
✓ Squeeze collects no personal data of any kind. There is no account system, no login, and no analytics.
Squeeze runs entirely in your browser. There is no backend server processing your requests, no database storing your activity, and no logging of any kind.
2. Your Images
Images you upload to Squeeze are processed exclusively on your own device using browser-native APIs (FileReader, HTMLCanvasElement). They are:
  • Never transmitted to any server or third party
  • Never stored in local storage, IndexedDB, or any persistent browser storage
  • Held in temporary memory only for the duration of the compression operation
  • Immediately eligible for garbage collection once you close the tab or navigate away
3. Cookies & Tracking
Squeeze sets no cookies and uses no tracking scripts, pixels, or fingerprinting techniques. There is no analytics library, no advertising SDK, and no session tracking of any kind.
✓ No cookies. No trackers. No fingerprinting. Ever.
4. Third Parties
Squeeze loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com) and example images from Unsplash (images.unsplash.com). These requests are made by your browser directly to those services and are subject to their respective privacy policies. No image data you upload is shared with these services.
5. Children's Privacy
Squeeze does not collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Because no personal information is collected at any point, Squeeze is inherently compliant with COPPA and similar regulations.
6. Changes to This Policy
If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised "last updated" date. Because we collect no data, any changes are unlikely to affect you in any meaningful way.
7. Contact
If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out via the Contact page.
